How to Make Tonkatsu

Tonkatsu is a Japanese fried pork cutlet. It is crispy outside and juicy inside.

Ingredients:
pork loin, salt, pepper, flour, egg, panko breadcrumbs, cooking oil, cabbage, tonkatsu sauce

Steps:

1. Hit the pork lightly with your hand to make it flat.

2. Put salt and pepper on both sides of the pork.

3. Cover the pork with flour, then egg, then panko.

4. Heat oil in a deep pan to 170 degrees.

5. Fry the pork for 4 minutes on each side until golden.

6. Cut the tonkatsu into pieces and serve with cabbage. Enjoy!
