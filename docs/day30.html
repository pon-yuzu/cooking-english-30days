How to Make Matcha Pudding

Matcha pudding is a sweet and creamy Japanese dessert. It has a beautiful green color and a gentle tea taste.

Ingredients:
matcha powder, milk, sugar, gelatin powder, hot water, cream, vanilla extract

Steps:

1. Mix the matcha powder and a little hot water in a cup until smooth.

2. Heat the milk and sugar in a pot, but do not boil it.

3. Add the gelatin powder to the warm milk and stir well.

4. Pour the matcha mix into the pot and stir again.

5. Put the mix into small cups or glasses.

6. Cool them in the fridge for three hours, then add cream on top. Enjoy!
