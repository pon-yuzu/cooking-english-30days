How to Make Shumai

Shumai are small steamed dumplings from Japan. They are soft and very tasty.

Ingredients:
ground pork, onion, corn starch, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, salt, pepper, shumai wrappers, green peas

Steps:

1. Cut the onion into very small pieces and mix with corn starch.

2. Put the pork, onion, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, salt, and pepper in a bowl and mix well.

3. Hold one wrapper in your hand and put some meat in the middle.

4. Close the wrapper around the meat but leave the top open.

5. Put one green pea on top of each shumai.

6. Steam the shumai for about ten minutes until they are cooked. Enjoy!
