How to Make Chawanmushi

Chawanmushi is a Japanese egg custard. It is soft, warm, and very gentle.

Ingredients:
eggs, dashi stock, soy sauce, salt, chicken, shrimp, mushroom, mitsuba (or parsley)

Steps:

1. Mix two eggs, dashi stock, a little soy sauce, and salt in a bowl.

2. Strain the egg mix through a net to make it smooth.

3. Put small pieces of chicken, shrimp, and mushroom in cups.

4. Pour the egg mix into the cups slowly.

5. Cover the cups with foil and steam for fifteen minutes on low heat.

6. Put mitsuba or parsley on top and serve. Enjoy!
