How to Make Tsukemono

Tsukemono are Japanese pickled vegetables. Asazuke is a quick and light pickle. It is very easy and takes only 30 minutes.

Ingredients:
cucumber, cabbage, carrot, salt, kombu (dried kelp), ginger, chili flakes, rice vinegar

Steps:

1. Cut the cucumber, cabbage, and carrot into thin pieces.

2. Put all the vegetables in a big bowl.

3. Add salt, small pieces of kombu, and a little ginger.

4. Mix everything well with your hands.

5. Put a plate on top and something heavy on the plate.

6. Wait for 30 minutes, then take out the vegetables and put them on a plate. Enjoy!
