How to Make Udon

Udon are thick Japanese noodles. They are soft and chewy in hot soup.

Ingredients:
udon noodles, dashi stock, soy sauce, mirin, salt, green onion, kamaboko (fish cake), egg

Steps:

1. Boil water in a big pot and cook the udon noodles.

2. Make the soup by mixing dashi stock, soy sauce, mirin, and salt in another pot.

3. Heat the soup on medium heat until it is hot.

4. Drain the udon noodles and put them in bowls.

5. Pour the hot soup over the noodles.

6. Add green onion, fish cake, and an egg on top. Enjoy!
