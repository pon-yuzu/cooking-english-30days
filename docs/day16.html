How to Make Nikujaga

Nikujaga is a Japanese meat and potato stew. It is sweet and warm, and very good for cold days.

Ingredients:
thin beef slices, potatoes, onion, carrot, soy sauce, sugar, mirin, water, cooking oil

Steps:

1. Cut the potatoes and carrot into big pieces.

2. Slice the onion into thin pieces.

3. Heat oil in a big pot and cook the beef.

4. Add the onion, potatoes, and carrot to the pot.

5. Pour water, soy sauce, sugar, and mirin into the pot.

6. Cook on low heat for 20 minutes until soft. Enjoy!
