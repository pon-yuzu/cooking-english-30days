How to Make Ochazuke

Ochazuke is a simple Japanese dish. You pour hot green tea or dashi over rice. It is warm and easy to make.

Ingredients:
rice, green tea or dashi stock, salmon (cooked), nori (seaweed), wasabi, sesame seeds, green onion, umeboshi (pickled plum)

Steps:

1. Cook the rice and put it in a bowl.

2. Put the cooked salmon on top of the rice.

3. Add small pieces of nori and green onion on top.

4. Make hot green tea or dashi stock.

5. Pour the hot tea slowly over the rice.

6. Add wasabi and sesame seeds on top. Enjoy!
