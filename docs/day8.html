How to Make Ramen

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup. The warm broth is very comforting.

Ingredients:
ramen noodles, pork, egg, soy sauce, miso, garlic, green onion, nori, water, sesame oil

Steps:

1. Boil water in a big pot and cook the noodles for three minutes.

2. Make the broth with water, soy sauce, miso, and garlic in another pot.

3. Cook the pork in a pan with sesame oil until it is brown.

4. Boil an egg for seven minutes, then put it in cold water and peel it.

5. Put the noodles in a bowl and pour the hot broth over them.

6. Add the pork, egg, green onion, and nori on top. Enjoy!
