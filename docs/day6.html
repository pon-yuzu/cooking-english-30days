How to Make Kakuni

Kakuni is soft pork belly. It is sweet and very good.

Ingredients:
pork belly, soy sauce, sugar, sake, ginger, green onion, water, egg

Steps:

1. Cut the pork belly into big pieces.

2. Boil the pork in water for ten minutes, then take it out.

3. Put the pork, soy sauce, sugar, sake, ginger, and green onion in a pot.

4. Add water and cook on low heat for two hours.

5. Boil eggs and peel them, then put them in the pot for thirty minutes.

6. Serve the pork and eggs on a plate. Enjoy!
