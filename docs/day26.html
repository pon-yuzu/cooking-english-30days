How to Make Katsudon

Katsudon is a Japanese rice bowl with a fried pork cutlet and egg. It is a very popular comfort food in Japan.

Ingredients:
pork cutlet (tonkatsu), egg, onion, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, dashi stock, rice, green onion

Steps:

1. Cook the rice and put it in a bowl.

2. Cut the onion into thin pieces.

3. Mix the soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and dashi stock in a small pan.

4. Add the onion to the pan and cook for two minutes.

5. Put the pork cutlet on top of the onion and pour the beaten egg over it.

6. Cover the pan and cook for one minute, then put everything on the rice. Enjoy!
