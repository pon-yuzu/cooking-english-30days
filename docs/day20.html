How to Make Edamame

Edamame is boiled young soybeans. It is a simple and healthy Japanese snack.

Ingredients:
edamame in shells, water, salt

Steps:

1. Wash the edamame in cold water.

2. Put salt on the edamame and rub them with your hands.

3. Boil a big pot of water with some salt.

4. Add the edamame to the boiling water.

5. Cook for 4 minutes, then take them out.

6. Put a little more salt on top and eat them from the shell. Enjoy!
