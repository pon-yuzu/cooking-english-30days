How to Make Soba

Soba are thin Japanese noodles made from buckwheat. You can eat them hot or cold.

Ingredients:
dried soba noodles, water, soy sauce, mirin, dashi stock, green onion, wasabi, nori (seaweed)

Steps:

1. Boil a lot of water in a big pot.

2. Cook the soba noodles in the water for four or five minutes.

3. Drain the noodles and wash them in cold water.

4. Mix soy sauce, mirin, and dashi stock in a small pot and heat it.

5. Put the cold soba on a plate and cut green onion on top.

6. Serve with dipping sauce, wasabi, and nori on the side. Enjoy!
