How to Make Japanese Curry

Japanese curry is a thick and warm dish. Many Japanese people love it with rice.

Ingredients:
beef or chicken, potato, carrot, onion, curry roux, water, vegetable oil

Steps:

1. Cut the meat, potato, carrot, and onion into small pieces.

2. Heat oil in a big pot on medium heat.

3. Cook the onion until it is soft and light brown.

4. Add the meat, potato, and carrot and cook for three minutes.

5. Pour water into the pot and cook for twenty minutes.

6. Add the curry roux and stir until the sauce is thick. Enjoy!
