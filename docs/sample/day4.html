How to Make Chicken Nanban

Chicken Nanban is fried chicken with sweet vinegar sauce and tartar sauce. It comes from Miyazaki in Japan.

Ingredients:
chicken thigh, salt, pepper, flour, egg, vegetable oil, soy sauce, sugar, rice vinegar, mayonnaise, boiled egg, onion, pickle

Steps:

1. Cut the chicken into big pieces and add salt and pepper.

2. Cover the chicken with flour and then dip it in beaten egg.

3. Fry the chicken in hot oil until it is golden brown.

4. Make the sweet vinegar sauce by mixing soy sauce, sugar, and rice vinegar in a small pot.

5. Put the fried chicken into the sweet vinegar sauce for one minute.

6. Make tartar sauce by mixing mayonnaise, chopped boiled egg, onion, and pickle, and put it on top of the chicken. Enjoy!
