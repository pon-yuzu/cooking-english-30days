How to Make Gyudon

Gyudon is a Japanese beef bowl. Thin beef and onion are cooked in a sweet sauce and put on rice.

Ingredients:
thin beef slices, onion, rice, soy sauce, sugar, mirin, water, ginger

Steps:

1. Cook the rice in a rice cooker.

2. Slice the onion into thin pieces.

3. Mix soy sauce, sugar, mirin, and water in a pan.

4. Add the onion and ginger and cook for 3 minutes.

5. Put the beef in the pan and cook for 5 minutes.

6. Put the beef and sauce on the rice in a bowl. Enjoy!
