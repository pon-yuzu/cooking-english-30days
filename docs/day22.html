How to Make Tempura

Tempura is Japanese fried food. Shrimp and vegetables are covered in light batter.

Ingredients:
shrimp, sweet potato, eggplant, green beans, flour, egg, cold water, vegetable oil, soy sauce, dashi, grated daikon

Steps:

1. Peel the shrimp and cut the vegetables into pieces.

2. Mix flour, one egg, and very cold water in a bowl to make batter.

3. Heat a lot of oil in a deep pot to 180 degrees.

4. Dip the shrimp and vegetables in the batter.

5. Fry them in the oil for two or three minutes until golden.

6. Put them on paper to remove extra oil and serve with dipping sauce. Enjoy!
