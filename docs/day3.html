How to Make Karaage

Karaage is Japanese fried chicken. It is crispy outside and juicy inside.

Ingredients:
chicken thigh, soy sauce, sake, ginger, garlic, egg, corn starch, flour, vegetable oil, lemon

Steps:

1. Cut the chicken into bite-size pieces.

2. Mix soy sauce, sake, ginger, and garlic in a bowl.

3. Put the chicken in the bowl and wait for fifteen minutes.

4. Add egg and corn starch to the chicken and mix well.

5. Fry the chicken in hot oil until it is golden brown.

6. Serve on a plate with a piece of lemon. Enjoy!
