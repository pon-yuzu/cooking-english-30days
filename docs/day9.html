How to Make Onigiri

Onigiri is a Japanese rice ball. It is easy to make and great for lunch.

Ingredients:
cooked rice, salt, nori, tuna, mayonnaise, umeboshi, salmon

Steps:

1. Mix tuna and mayonnaise in a small bowl.

2. Wet your hands with water and put some salt on them.

3. Put warm rice on your hand and make a small hole in the middle.

4. Put the tuna, umeboshi, or salmon in the hole.

5. Close the rice and press it into a triangle shape with your hands.

6. Wrap the onigiri with nori and put it on a plate. Enjoy!
