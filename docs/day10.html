How to Make Miso Soup

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup. Japanese people drink it every day.

Ingredients:
miso paste, tofu, wakame seaweed, dashi stock, green onion, water

Steps:

1. Put water and dashi stock in a pot and heat it.

2. Cut the tofu into small cubes.

3. Put the wakame seaweed in water for five minutes to soften it.

4. Add the tofu and wakame to the pot when the water is hot.

5. Turn off the heat and add the miso paste, then stir well.

6. Pour the soup into bowls and add green onion on top. Enjoy!
