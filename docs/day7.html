How to Make Fried Rice

Fried rice is a quick and easy dish. You can use many kinds of vegetables and meat.

Ingredients:
cooked rice, egg, green onion, carrot, ham, soy sauce, salt, pepper, oil

Steps:

1. Cut the ham, carrot, and green onion into small pieces.

2. Heat oil in a pan on high heat.

3. Crack the egg into the pan and mix it quickly.

4. Add the rice and stir it well with the egg.

5. Put the ham, carrot, and green onion in the pan and cook for two minutes.

6. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper, then mix everything together. Enjoy!
