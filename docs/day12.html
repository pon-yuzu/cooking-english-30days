How to Make Teriyaki Chicken

Teriyaki chicken is a popular Japanese dish. The chicken has a sweet and shiny sauce.

Ingredients:
chicken thigh, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, vegetable oil, white sesame seeds

Steps:

1. Cut the chicken thigh into big pieces.

2. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat.

3. Put the chicken in the pan, skin side down, and cook for five minutes.

4. Turn the chicken over and cook for three more minutes.

5. Add soy sauce, mirin, and sugar to the pan.

6. Cook until the sauce is thick and shiny. Enjoy!
