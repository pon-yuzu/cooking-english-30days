How to Make Yakitori

Yakitori is Japanese grilled chicken on sticks. The sauce is sweet and salty.

Ingredients:
chicken thigh, green onion, soy sauce, sugar, mirin, sake, bamboo sticks

Steps:

1. Cut the chicken into small pieces.

2. Cut the green onion into short pieces.

3. Put chicken and green onion on the bamboo sticks.

4. Mix soy sauce, sugar, mirin, and sake in a small pot and boil.

5. Grill the sticks on a hot pan for 3 minutes on each side.

6. Brush the sauce on the chicken and grill one more minute. Enjoy!
