How to Make Takowasa

Takowasa is a Japanese snack of raw octopus with wasabi. It is a popular side dish at izakayas.

Ingredients:
raw octopus (sashimi grade), wasabi, soy sauce, salt, mirin, sesame seeds, shiso leaf

Steps:

1. Cut the raw octopus into small bite-size pieces.

2. Put the octopus pieces in a bowl.

3. Mix the wasabi, soy sauce, and a little mirin in a small cup.

4. Pour the wasabi sauce over the octopus.

5. Stir everything gently with chopsticks.

6. Put it on a plate with a shiso leaf and sesame seeds. Enjoy!
