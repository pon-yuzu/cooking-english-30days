How to Make Takoyaki

Takoyaki are small round balls from Osaka. They have octopus inside and are crispy outside.

Ingredients:
flour, egg, water, dashi powder, octopus, green onion, pickled ginger, takoyaki sauce, mayonnaise, bonito flakes, vegetable oil

Steps:

1. Mix flour, egg, water, and dashi powder in a big bowl to make the batter.

2. Cut the octopus, green onion, and pickled ginger into small pieces.

3. Heat oil in the takoyaki pan and pour the batter into each hole.

4. Drop a piece of octopus and some green onion into each hole.

5. Turn each ball with a pick when the bottom is cooked.

6. Cook until all the balls are round and golden brown. Put sauce, mayonnaise, and bonito flakes on top. Enjoy!
