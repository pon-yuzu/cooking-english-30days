How to Make Oyakodon

Oyakodon is a Japanese rice bowl with chicken and egg. The name means 'parent and child.'

Ingredients:
chicken thigh, onion, eggs, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, dashi stock, cooked rice, green onion

Steps:

1. Cut the chicken thigh and onion into small pieces.

2. Mix soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and dashi stock in a small pan.

3. Cook the onion and chicken in the sauce on medium heat.

4. Beat two or three eggs in a bowl.

5. Pour the eggs over the chicken and cook for one minute.

6. Put the chicken and egg on top of hot rice in a bowl. Enjoy!
